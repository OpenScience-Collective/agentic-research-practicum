## Year-on-year rationale

**Year 1 ($125,000 direct, 5 modules)** funds:
- Pipeline validation work on N=20 → N=50 (months 1-6), light personnel and modest cloud-compute use.
- Initial group-level Aim 1 analysis ramp at N≈160 (months 7-12), moderate compute load.
- Open Science Framework (OSF) pre-registration (month 6) and Aim 1 manuscript drafting (month 12).

**Year 2 ($150,000 direct, 6 modules)** funds:
- Full Aim 1 group analysis and manuscript submission (months 7-12 of project; falls into Year 2 calendar months 7-12 = project months 13-18 if a calendar-year cycle; the front-loading of effort reflects the analysis timeline rather than the calendar).
- Aim 2 LLR-partialled GLM and falsification-region localization (months 13-18).
- Cross-cohort replication on the adult electroencephalographic and functional magnetic resonance imaging (EEG-fMRI) cohort (`telesford-2023-eeg-fmri-naturalistic`).
- OpenNeuro and NEMAR deposition (month 18).
- Aim 2 manuscript submission and R01 follow-on preparation (months 19-24).
- Statistical consultant fee (Year 2 only).
- Two open-access publication fees.

See `justification.md` for the narrative tying each module category to the timeline in Approach Section 5.
