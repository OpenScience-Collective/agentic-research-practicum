## A. Personal statement

I am positioned to lead the proposed R21 because I combine three skill sets that the project requires: (1) hands-on expertise with the EEGLAB / AMICA / ICLabel pipeline at the level of contributor, not just user; (2) co-development of the BIDS-EEG and Hierarchical Event Descriptor (HED) infrastructure that the proposed analyses depend on (`hermes-2023-hed-schema`, where I am a co-author of the HED 8.3.0 schema paper, and `robbins-2021-hed` for the HED foundations); and (3) prior re-analysis work on Healthy Brain Network (HBN) EEG releases, including the analysis epic that produced the pipeline pilot results cited in the Approach. ## C. Contributions to science

**1. Open infrastructure for naturalistic and event-related EEG analysis (HED, BIDS-EEG, EEGLAB).** I co-developed the Hierarchical Event Descriptor (HED) 8.3.0 schema for EEG event annotation, an outcome of multi-year collaborative work across the EEGLAB and BIDS communities. HED 8.3.0 supports the per-shot animacy annotation that the proposed R21 uses to define its Aim 1 and Aim 2 contrasts.

Key publications:
