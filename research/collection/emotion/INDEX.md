# Emotion Strand Collection Index

Phase 1 paper-card corpus for the emotion strand of the HBN ThePresent boy-shot vs puppy-shot literature review. Twenty-five entries across the seven brief categories. The puppy-shot interpretation depends on Category 5 (pet/animal-evoked affective response); the cohort is children and adolescents (Healthy Brain Network, ages 5-21), so adult-only studies carry an explicit developmental-justification note on each card.

## Category 1, Affective dynamics in cinematic stimuli

- [saarimaki-2016-discrete-emotions](./saarimaki-2016-discrete-emotions/card.md): MVPA evidence for distinguishable basic-emotion patterns during emotional film viewing and imagery (`relevance: high`, 2016).
- [nummenmaa-2012-emotion-synchrony](./nummenmaa-2012-emotion-synchrony/card.md): emotion-driven inter-subject brain synchronisation in midline cortex during film clips (`relevance: high`, 2012).
- [kragel-2019-emonet-visual](./kragel-2019-emonet-visual/card.md): EmoNet deep-learning model shows that emotion schemas are encoded in visual cortex, not only in limbic regions (`relevance: medium`, 2019).
- [cowen-keltner-2017-27-emotions](./cowen-keltner-2017-27-emotions/card.md): self-report taxonomy of 27 emotion categories elicited by short videos (`relevance: medium`, 2017).

## Category 2, Social cognition and Theory of Mind during movie watching

- [saxe-kanwisher-2003-tpj-tom](./saxe-kanwisher-2003-tpj-tom/card.md): right temporo-parietal junction selective for theory-of-mind reasoning (`relevance: medium`, 2003).
- [richardson-saxe-2018-social-brain-development](./richardson-saxe-2018-social-brain-development/card.md): Pixar short film localises ToM and pain networks across ages 3-12, showing developmental refinement (`relevance: high`, 2018).

## Category 3, Amygdala, vmPFC, insula responses to affective film clips

- [ledoux-2010-amygdala-many-roads](./ledoux-2010-amygdala-many-roads/card.md): Pessoa-Adolphs canonical update of LeDoux's low-road, arguing for cortically integrated amygdala evaluation (`relevance: medium`, 2010).
- [lindquist-2012-emotion-meta-analysis](./lindquist-2012-emotion-meta-analysis/card.md): meta-analysis showing distributed emotion networks rather than one-to-one localisation (`relevance: high`, 2012).
- [sergerie-2008-amygdala-meta-analysis](./sergerie-2008-amygdala-meta-analysis/card.md): amygdala meta-analysis confirming bivalent response, with face stimuli most consistent (`relevance: medium`, 2008).
- [etkin-2011-acc-mpfc-emotion](./etkin-2011-acc-mpfc-emotion/card.md): dorsal-versus-ventral mPFC dissociation for appraisal versus regulation of emotion (`relevance: medium`, 2011).
- [wager-2013-neurologic-pain-signature](./wager-2013-neurologic-pain-signature/card.md): canonical neurologic pain signature, foundational for distributed multivariate signatures of affect (`relevance: medium`, 2013).

## Category 4, Inter-subject correlation (ISC) and reliability driven by emotional content

- [petroni-cohen-2018-isc-naturalistic-videos](./petroni-cohen-2018-isc-naturalistic-videos/card.md): EEG inter-subject correlation during naturalistic videos varies with viewer age and sex; child-cohort relevant (Petroni, Cohen, Ai et al., `relevance: high`, 2018).
- [schmalzle-2020-coupled-brains](./schmalzle-2020-coupled-brains/card.md): media-psychology theoretical synthesis of ISC as audience captivation metric (`relevance: medium`, 2020).
- [kauttonen-2015-cinematic-fmri](./kauttonen-2015-cinematic-fmri/card.md): methodological template for linking multi-level cinematic features to fMRI activity (`relevance: medium`, 2015).

## Category 5, Pet / animal-evoked affective response

- [stoeckel-2014-mother-child-dog](./stoeckel-2014-mother-child-dog/card.md): mothers viewing own child versus own dog activate partly shared affiliative network with VTA/SN child-specific and fusiform dog-specific (`relevance: high`, 2014).
- [glocker-2009-baby-schema](./glocker-2009-baby-schema/card.md): baby-schema parametric modulation of nucleus accumbens reward response in nulliparous women (`relevance: high`, 2009).
- [borgi-2014-baby-schema-children](./borgi-2014-baby-schema-children/card.md): children aged 3-6 show baby-schema cuteness effect for human infants and for puppy/kitten faces (`relevance: high`, 2014; developmental anchor for puppy-shot).

## Category 6, Frontal alpha asymmetry and EEG affective markers

- [davidson-2000-affective-style](./davidson-2000-affective-style/card.md): canonical approach-withdrawal frontal-alpha-asymmetry framework (`relevance: medium`, 2000).
- [coan-allen-2004-frontal-asymmetry](./coan-allen-2004-frontal-asymmetry/card.md): trait-versus-state asymmetry capability model (`relevance: medium`, 2004).
- [reznik-allen-2018-frontal-asymmetry](./reznik-allen-2018-frontal-asymmetry/card.md): updated review with smaller effect sizes and methodological cautions (`relevance: medium`, 2018).
- [schubring-schupp-2023-alpha-emotion](./schubring-schupp-2023-alpha-emotion/card.md): comprehensive review of alpha-band oscillations during emotional picture perception (`relevance: high`, 2023).

## Category 7, Empathy and mentalizing in narrative consumption

- [mar-2011-narrative-social-cognition](./mar-2011-narrative-social-cognition/card.md): narrative comprehension and theory-of-mind share neural machinery (`relevance: medium`, 2011).
- [tamir-2015-fiction-default-network](./tamir-2015-fiction-default-network/card.md): fiction reading preferentially engages default-mode network and lifetime exposure predicts engagement (`relevance: medium`, 2016).
- [singer-2004-empathy-pain](./singer-2004-empathy-pain/card.md): empathy for pain shares the affective but not sensory components of the pain network (`relevance: medium`, 2004).
- [zaki-ochsner-2012-empathy-neural](./zaki-ochsner-2012-empathy-neural/card.md): two-system (experience sharing plus mental-state attribution) model of empathy (`relevance: medium`, 2012).
