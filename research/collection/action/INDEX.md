# Action Strand Collection Index

Phase 2 action-strand corpus for the HBN ThePresent boy-shot vs puppy-shot event-related spectral perturbation (ERSP) review. 18 entries across 6 categories. Compiled 2026-05-06.

## 1. Biological motion perception

- [johansson-1973-biological-motion](./johansson-1973-biological-motion/card.md): Foundational point-light walker paradigm; vector model for articulated kinematics. (`relevance: medium`, 1973)
- [saygin-2007-sts-lesions](./saygin-2007-sts-lesions/card.md): Voxel-based lesion-symptom mapping; biological motion requires both posterior STS and ventral premotor cortex. (`relevance: high`, 2007)
- [klin-2009-biological-motion-autism](./klin-2009-biological-motion-autism/card.md): Toddlers with autism orient to audiovisual contingency rather than upright biological motion. (`relevance: medium`, 2009)

## 2. Action observation networks (parietal-premotor, mirror system)

- [rizzolatti-2004-mirror-neuron-system](./rizzolatti-2004-mirror-neuron-system/card.md): Annual-Review consolidation of macaque F5/PF and human IFG/IPL mirror system. (`relevance: high`, 2004)
- [kilner-2007-predictive-coding-mirror](./kilner-2007-predictive-coding-mirror/card.md): Recasts mirror neurons as a hierarchical Bayesian predictive-coding system over intention, goal, kinematics. (`relevance: high`, 2007)
- [iacoboni-2009-mirror-empathy](./iacoboni-2009-mirror-empathy/card.md): Annual-Review extension of mirror system to imitation, empathy, and emotion contagion. (`relevance: medium`, 2009)
