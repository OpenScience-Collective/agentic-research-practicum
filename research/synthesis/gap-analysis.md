# Gap Analysis

Three-column accounting of how the four-strand corpus covers the principal topics needed by the Healthy Brain Network electroencephalography (HBN-EEG) ThePresent boy-shot vs puppy-shot event-related spectral perturbation (ERSP) project, followed by a concrete gap list. Gaps are statements of absence: the corpus does not contain entries that do X for our paradigm, and the cards listed bound the negative space. Contradictions across strands are not gaps and live in `science-map.md` under "Open questions".

Abbreviations: event-related spectral perturbation (ERSP), inter-subject correlation (ISC), log luminance ratio (LLR), default-mode network (DMN), theory of mind (ToM), independent component analysis (ICA), Healthy Brain Network (HBN), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), magnetoencephalography (MEG), intracranial electroencephalography (iEEG), generalized linear model (GLM), Pixar short, fixation-related potential (FRP).

## Three-column coverage table

The two prior-effort axes are: prior cinematic-fMRI work (the most-cited tradition in the corpus) and prior naturalistic-EEG work (the closest electrophysiological precedent). The third column is what neither covers and what the planned ThePresent ERSP analysis can therefore distinctively address.

## Concrete gap list

### Gap 1: Child-cohort EEG ERSP at shot onsets in animation

**Established by**: [petroni-cohen-2018-isc-naturalistic-videos](../collection/emotion/petroni-cohen-2018-isc-naturalistic-videos/card.md), [richardson-saxe-2018-social-brain-development](../collection/emotion/richardson-saxe-2018-social-brain-development/card.md), [vanderwal-2015-inscapes](../collection/language/vanderwal-2015-inscapes/card.md), [dmochowski-2012-correlated-eeg-engagement](../collection/psychophysics/dmochowski-2012-correlated-eeg-engagement/card.md), [nentwich-2023-semantic-novelty-cuts](../collection/psychophysics/nentwich-2023-semantic-novelty-cuts/card.md).

The corpus has whole-clip developmental EEG-ISC, child-cohort fMRI of Pixar shorts, adult iEEG of film cuts, and adult scalp-EEG ISC of naturalistic stimuli. It does not contain a single paper-card that reports per-shot ERSP at the 0-500 ms post-onset window in a child cohort viewing animation. ThePresent analysis at ds005507 is the natural-fit setting for this measurement; it is also the project's primary hypothesis.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will state that the corpus contains zero per-shot ERSP studies in a child cohort during animation, and that ThePresent at HBN-EEG R3 (n ~ 184 in the local 100 Hz working set) is positioned to be the first.

### Gap 2: LLR as a continuous regressor in EEG ERSP

**Established by**: [kauttonen-2015-cinematic-fmri](../collection/emotion/kauttonen-2015-cinematic-fmri/card.md), [nentwich-2023-semantic-novelty-cuts](../collection/psychophysics/nentwich-2023-semantic-novelty-cuts/card.md), [kaneshiro-2021-music-eeg-isc](../collection/psychophysics/kaneshiro-2021-music-eeg-isc/card.md), [bartels-zeki-2008-natural-vision-mt](../collection/psychophysics/bartels-zeki-2008-natural-vision-mt/card.md), [tobimatsu-celesia-2006-vep-pathophysiology](../collection/psychophysics/tobimatsu-celesia-2006-vep-pathophysiology/card.md).

The corpus has cinematic-fMRI work that uses luminance and contrast as regressors, an iEEG demonstration that motion outperforms luminance/contrast in occipitoparietal cortex, and an envelope-control auditory analogue. It does not include any scalp-EEG study that uses log luminance ratio per shot as a continuous nuisance regressor in a group-level GLM. ThePresent's `shot_events.tsv` LLR column makes this immediately tractable.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will introduce LLR as the simplest available low-level regressor for shot-locked EEG ERSP and will note that the corpus contains no precedent for this specific use, leaving open whether motion-energy regressors (per Nentwich 2023) should be added.

### Gap 3: Pet- or animal-evoked affective EEG, especially in children

**Established by**: [stoeckel-2014-mother-child-dog](../collection/emotion/stoeckel-2014-mother-child-dog/card.md), [glocker-2009-baby-schema](../collection/emotion/glocker-2009-baby-schema/card.md), [borgi-2014-baby-schema-children](../collection/emotion/borgi-2014-baby-schema-children/card.md).

The corpus contains exactly three primary papers on pet- or animal-evoked affective response: one adult fMRI of mothers viewing own child versus own dog photographs, one adult fMRI of baby-schema in infant photographs, and one behavioral plus eye-tracking study of baby-schema in children including puppy and kitten faces. None record EEG, none use a moving stimulus, and none simultaneously address the developmental and the species crossover question. The puppy-shot interpretation in ThePresent rests on this small set, which is the smallest theme in the corpus per `science-map.md`.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will identify pet-evoked affective EEG in children as the smallest-coverage area in the corpus and will interpret any boy-vs-puppy ERSP difference at central or frontal electrode clusters as the first such observation. It will explicitly avoid claims about identity-level pair-bonding circuitry (the [stoeckel-2014-mother-child-dog](../collection/emotion/stoeckel-2014-mother-child-dog/card.md) finding requires the participants' own pets).

### Gap 4: Silent-narrative ERSP at 0-500 ms event boundaries

**Established by**: [castelli-2000-animations-mentalising](../collection/language/castelli-2000-animations-mentalising/card.md), [vanderwal-2015-inscapes](../collection/language/vanderwal-2015-inscapes/card.md), [naci-2014-suspenseful-movie](../collection/language/naci-2014-suspenseful-movie/card.md), [richardson-saxe-2018-social-brain-development](../collection/emotion/richardson-saxe-2018-social-brain-development/card.md), [lankinen-2014-meg-movie-consistency](../collection/language/lankinen-2014-meg-movie-consistency/card.md), [magliano-2011-continuity-editing](../collection/language/magliano-2011-continuity-editing/card.md), [benyakov-2018-hippocampal-film-editor](../collection/language/benyakov-2018-hippocampal-film-editor/card.md).

Silent-stimulus naturalistic-narrative neural correlates are well covered in fMRI (Heider-Simmel, Inscapes, Hitchcock, Partly Cloudy) and have one MEG analogue with a silent-visual condition ([lankinen-2014-meg-movie-consistency](../collection/language/lankinen-2014-meg-movie-consistency/card.md) with N = 8 adults). The corpus contains no scalp-EEG ERSP study locked to event boundaries in a silent narrative. The Magliano-Zacks behavioral foundation establishes that silent film cuts modulate event segmentation but does not connect to a band-limited spectral measurement.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will frame ThePresent's 56-shot annotated stimulus as the first scalp-EEG ERSP probe of silent-narrative event boundaries, with the silent-stimulus framing intentionally chosen to factor out the speech-driven regressor families that dominate Theme 9 in `science-map.md`.

### Gap 5: Cross-strand integration into a single per-shot ERSP model

**Established by**: [kauttonen-2015-cinematic-fmri](../collection/emotion/kauttonen-2015-cinematic-fmri/card.md), [nentwich-2023-semantic-novelty-cuts](../collection/psychophysics/nentwich-2023-semantic-novelty-cuts/card.md), [madsen-parra-2022-brain-heart-eyes-sync](../collection/psychophysics/madsen-parra-2022-brain-heart-eyes-sync/card.md), [dmochowski-2012-correlated-eeg-engagement](../collection/psychophysics/dmochowski-2012-correlated-eeg-engagement/card.md), [petroni-cohen-2018-isc-naturalistic-videos](../collection/emotion/petroni-cohen-2018-isc-naturalistic-videos/card.md).

Existing studies decompose naturalistic-stimulus responses along one dimension at a time: low-level visual features in fMRI (Kauttonen, Bartels-Zeki), motion versus cuts versus saccades in iEEG (Nentwich), brain-body sync at the clip level (Madsen and Parra), or whole-clip ISC age effects (Petroni). No card combines low-level (LLR, motion energy), action (boy/puppy presence), and affective regressors in one shot-locked EEG GLM. The planned Phase 5 ThePresent analysis is constructed to do this.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will name this as a methodological gap and will outline the proposed multi-regressor structure for the group-level analysis, with LLR as the locked low-level covariate and condition (boy-only vs puppy-only) as the locked categorical predictor.

### Gap 6: Free-viewing animation EEG without eye-movement coregistration

**Established by**: [dimigen-2011-frp-natural-reading](../collection/psychophysics/dimigen-2011-frp-natural-reading/card.md), [ploechl-2012-eye-eeg-artifact-correction](../collection/psychophysics/ploechl-2012-eye-eeg-artifact-correction/card.md), [dimigen-ehinger-2021-deconvolution-eye-eeg](../collection/psychophysics/dimigen-ehinger-2021-deconvolution-eye-eeg/card.md), [madsen-parra-2022-brain-heart-eyes-sync](../collection/psychophysics/madsen-parra-2022-brain-heart-eyes-sync/card.md), [dorr-2010-eye-movements-natural-movies](../collection/psychophysics/dorr-2010-eye-movements-natural-movies/card.md).

The corpus's free-viewing EEG methodology assumes simultaneous eye tracking, which HBN-EEG R3 lacks. The Dimigen-Ehinger deconvolution framework explicitly requires saccade onsets; Plöchl ICA-based artifact rejection achieves AUC > 0.99 against expert labels but only with eye-tracker timing. There is no published guidance in the corpus for shot-locked EEG analysis on a developmental cohort with no eye tracker. This is a gap in established methodology applied to ThePresent's specific data shape.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will name this as a methodological boundary, document the limits it places on per-shot deconvolution, and propose ICA-only artifact rejection (per the EEGLAB AMICA + ICLabel pipeline) as the operating compromise. The 500 Hz validation pass on full R3 will not change this gap because eye tracking is not available there either.

### Gap 7: Mu-band action-observation EEG to animated agents

**Established by**: [hari-1998-mep-action-observation](../collection/action/hari-1998-mep-action-observation/card.md), [pineda-2005-mu-rhythm-mirror](../collection/action/pineda-2005-mu-rhythm-mirror/card.md), [oberman-2006-mu-mirror-autism](../collection/action/oberman-2006-mu-mirror-autism/card.md), [castelli-2000-heider-simmel](../collection/action/castelli-2000-heider-simmel/card.md), [johansson-1973-biological-motion](../collection/action/johansson-1973-biological-motion/card.md).

Mu-rhythm and beta-rebound suppression have been validated for hand-action observation in adults but never for character-animation action observation. Heider-Simmel fMRI shows that animated triangles engage the social-cognition network, but the EEG mu-band signature for animated-agent action has no card in the corpus. The corpus also contains no EEG study of animal action observation (puppy in motion).

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will hypothesize mu-band ERD for shots with prominent character action and will frame this as an extrapolation from human-hand-action mu suppression to animated-agent action, naming the absence of a direct precedent.

### Gap 8: Frontal alpha asymmetry at sub-second timescales during dynamic naturalistic stimuli

**Established by**: [davidson-2000-affective-style](../collection/emotion/davidson-2000-affective-style/card.md), [coan-allen-2004-frontal-asymmetry](../collection/emotion/coan-allen-2004-frontal-asymmetry/card.md), [reznik-allen-2018-frontal-asymmetry](../collection/emotion/reznik-allen-2018-frontal-asymmetry/card.md), [schubring-schupp-2023-alpha-emotion](../collection/emotion/schubring-schupp-2023-alpha-emotion/card.md).

Frontal alpha asymmetry has been validated as an EEG affective marker in adults at the seconds-to-minutes scale, with smaller and reliability-limited effects in updated meta-analyses. There is no card in the corpus that applies asymmetry analysis to per-event sub-second windows during a continuous naturalistic stimulus, and none in a developmental cohort viewing film.

**Proposed Phase 3 commitment**: The direction paper will treat shot-locked frontal asymmetry as exploratory rather than confirmatory, citing the contradiction between [davidson-2000-affective-style](../collection/emotion/davidson-2000-affective-style/card.md) and [reznik-allen-2018-frontal-asymmetry](../collection/emotion/reznik-allen-2018-frontal-asymmetry/card.md) and the absence of any cards demonstrating sub-second asymmetry effects during naturalistic film.
